Imagine a room in which you have a red button in front of you. This button, if pressed, will slowly degrade society and slow down our advancements with every press of a button. It may seem like a no-brainer to not press this button but it may not be apparent to others. This happens in the world of Frankenstein, but instead of a red button, there are biases. These two seemingly things that seemingly don't correlate are in fact the same thing. Both are unnecessary and optional. Both are terrible for society as a whole. So next time a bias pops into your head, think about the red button.
